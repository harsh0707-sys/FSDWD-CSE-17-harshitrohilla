    • Flipkart is one of India's largest e-commerce platforms, founded in 2007. It began as an online bookstore but soon expanded to sell a wide range of products, including electronics, clothing, home goods, and more. Flipkart is known for offering competitive prices, frequent sales events, and fast delivery services, especially in urban areas. It also provides services like cash on delivery, easy returns, and various payment options, making online shopping more accessible to a wide audience in India. Flipkart was acquired by Walmart in 2018, strengthening its position in the Indian market.
